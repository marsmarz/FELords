Marth

The prince of Altea who fights for his homeland and deeply values his friends. After the War of Shadows and the War of Heroes, he becomes the first king of the Archanean Alliance.

Marth appears in Shadow Dragon and the Blade of Light on the NES, Mystery of the Emblem on the SNES, Shadow Dragon on the DS, New Mystery of the Emblem: Heroes of Light and Shadow on the DS, Heroes for mobile, and Warriors for the Switch.
