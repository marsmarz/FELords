Fire Emblem: Genealogy of the Holy War is set within the continent of Jugdral.

The game consists of twelve total chapters (ten numbered chapters along with a prologue and endgame chapter).

The game sets itself apart from other Fire Emblem games due to its unique item system.
It was also the first game in the series to include two separate parts of the story as well as child units with an inheritance system.
